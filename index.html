Thanks to Cryptocurrency, We have entered the era of the Web 3.0, and there is no looking back.

Kaleidoscope is a exclusive invite only online grassroots movement. A decentralized global cooperative of freelancers, which by the token holders votes will be able to use the DAO's assets to be invested in the production of decentralized applications aka Dapps, as well as personal usages to further our movement. This is a way for the DAO to hire individuals who can choose to stick to the their specialty fields(or learn new fields) and make a income while doing so.Everyone works their own hours and chooses their own gigs.The goal is to be an online global community that no one controls and no one can buy in. We are looking for programmers, graphic designers, web devs , smart contract devs, video editors, photographers, writers/editors, social media managers and anything related with online development,promotion and most importantly SECURITY .
